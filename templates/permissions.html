Permission Groups

A group grants a set of capabilities over a set of machines. A person's effective access is the union of every group they belong to.

Addresses in ALLOWED_EMAILS are break-glass superusers: they hold every capability over every machine regardless of the groups below, and they can always sign in. That is what lets you create the first group here, and what stops a mistake on this page from locking everyone out. Everyone else must be in a group to sign in at all.
